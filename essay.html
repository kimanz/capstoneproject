Photographic Essay: Water Scarcity among African Communities

1. The Daily Struggle for water

Women and children frequently walk for long distances to fetch water from a distant source due to scarcity of water supply points.

2. Contaminated Water Sources

African community members engage in collecting water from a contaminated source which contributes to various health issues.

3. Impact of contaminated water on Health

Children suffering from waterborne diseases after taking contaminated water due to lack of access to clean water.

4. Innovative solutions towards water scarcity

Community-driven initiatives such as rainwater harvesting and well construction helping alleviate water scarcity.

5. Creating Awareness towards water solutions

Local activists raising awareness and advocating for clean water access as a basic human right to the African community members.
